Our Services

Bount is more than just a search engine. We offer a variety of services to help you find, organize, and manage the information you need.

  • Search:

    Our powerful search algorithms can help you find exactly what you're looking for, whether it's on the web, in videos, images, or other sources.

  • Personalization:

    Bount can personalize your search results based on your preferences and interests, so you can get the information that matters most to you.

  • Bookmarks:

    Bount allows you to bookmark your favorite websites, so you can easily access them again later.

  • History:

    Bount keeps track of your search history, so you can easily revisit previous searches and find information that you may have forgotten.

  • Privacy:

    Bount is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not track your searches or collect any personal data.
